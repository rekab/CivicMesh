Welcome to CivicMesh

This WiFi doesn’t connect to the internet. It connects you to the people around you. You can read and post messages on a local community board, shared over radio.

No websites, email, or apps here. Just local communication. Delivery is best-effort.

How it works

Mesh Network

Small radios across the region relay messages to each other. No cell towers, no ISPs, no central owner.

This Hotspot

You're connected via WiFi to a local hub. On-site channels stay here. Mesh channels reach the wider network.

Community First

Messages are public. Be helpful. Be brief. Be kind.
